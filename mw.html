Research

User Interviews

We conducted 5 interviews with prospective business owners, to understand how to increase retention with new vendors.

Research Question: How can we improve the vendors experience for selling products online so they can increase their revenue?

These were the main takeaways from our user interviews:

Users were intimidated by setting up delivery services and felt discouraged selling online because of processing fees and the time and expenses it takes to build a website

Users found that social media was an easy and accessible starting point to start selling online and allowed users to reach a wider audience that is not reliant on foot traffic
